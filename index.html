Skills

Jack of All Trades

My greatest skill is my ability to understand several fields at once: computer science, human interaction, and graphical aesthetics. My range of skills makes me a great mediator, and enables me to quickly get a general picture of both technical and creative challenges.

I have a broad knowledge within the field of iPhone app development. Initiating and facilitating the Copenhagen developer community has been my passion for over four years, which has granted me great understanding of the people and opportunities within the field.

Coding

I like to build things, both in Photoshop and in code, My focus is primarily on front-end development. I can turn PSDs into great HTML/CSS, and make cool things happen with jQuery.

I understand the C programming language, the principles of object oriented programming, the prototypical inheritance paradigm of JavaScript, and the importance of committing changes
to Github.

Interaction

I am passionate about interaction design, I find it more important than the graphical appeal, your product can have a beautiful face, but if people cannot use it, what is it worth?

Interaction design is important on both desktop and mobile, the hard challenge of interaction design often lies between predictability and productivity. Interaction design becomes art when both are achieved.

Design

I love to design webpages and iPhone applications. Spending time on finding the right spacing, colors, curves, and contrasts, is really worth all the extra effort.

I can draw beautiful UI elements and combine several graphical elements into a appealing composition such as a website or an iPhone application.
